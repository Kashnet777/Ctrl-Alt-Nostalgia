Evidence 002 · Historical Division

The Lore™

The complete friendship timeline · From the beginning

A documentary about how two random classmates somehow ended up collecting years' worth of conversations, inside jokes, business ideas, and enough lore™ to confuse absolutely everyone else. The official record. Everything that happened, in the order it happened, as best as can be reconstructed given everyone's questionable memory.
Chapter 0 · Two Random Classmates
🎬 December 2022
Status: "Who even are you?"
Every friendship has an origin story. Some begin through common interests. Some through a shared hobby. Ours started with a lab file. And normal stuff. Do I even need to remind you? The first chats were exactly what you'd expect during pre-board season.
"Lab file bhej de."
  • Lab files
  • Uniform confusion
  • Exam preparation
  • Roll numbers
  • Science discussions
  • Homework panic
Nothing extraordinary. Yet between those practical messages were tiny jokes. You teased me. You'd ask me something about what happened in class because I usually knew what the teachers had announced (maybe, Idk). And that prank which ended up with me saying "BHUPPP OR BHAPPPP" (I don't know which one is right 😅) At the time, it was just another school conversation. Looking back... it was Chapter One.
Archive status: recovered · Context: surprisingly clear · Classification: foundational
Chapter I · From Classmate to Friend
🌱 January – March 2023
School was still the reason we talked. Almost every conversation had a purpose. Need notes? Need homework? Need to know what the teacher announced? Message. But slowly... the conversations stopped ending after the answer. One question somehow became five. Homework somehow became jokes. Without either of us noticing, we stopped texting because we needed something... and started texting because we were just bored.
Exact turning point: unrecovered · Both parties confirm it happened
Chapter II · Comfort Update v1.0
🌼 April – June 2023
Nothing huge happened. And that's exactly why this chapter matters. There wasn't one dramatic moment where we suddenly became friends. Instead... it was hundreds of completely ordinary conversations. Questions. Homework. Random jokes. School complaints. The kind of moments that don't seem important until you look back years later.
Significance: understated · Impact: total
Chapter III · The Classroom DLC
☀️ July – September 2023
By now we already knew each other's personalities. (Which turned out to be completely wrong, when I see it today.) Who replied instantly. Who forgot things. Who'd probably know what happened in class. School was still the common setting... but definitely not the only topic anymore.
First personality assessments on record · Both assessments: wildly inaccurate
Chapter IV · Friendship Becomes the Default
🍂 October – December 2023
By the end of the year, texting each other no longer needed a reason. Sometimes there was a reason. Sometimes there wasn't. Both worked equally well.
Reason required to initiate conversation: none · Status: default mode activated
Chapter V · Welcome to Class 12
📚 January – March 2024
Projects. Practicals. Tests. Teachers suddenly remembering that Boards existed. The chats somehow balanced between:
  • School panic
  • Random memes
  • Conversations that had absolutely nothing to do with either
Academic relevance of chats: declining · Chat frequency: increasing
Chapter VI · Randomness Starts Winning
🌤 April – June 2024
Ironically... the busier school became, the less we actually talked about school. One reel became twenty messages. One random thought became an hour-long discussion. The conversations were slowly becoming their own thing.
School discussed: less · Everything else discussed: significantly more
Chapter VII · The Inside Joke Factory Opens
🎭 July – September 2024
This was where our own language™ started forming. References. Callbacks. Running jokes. The type of conversations where someone else would ask:
"What are you two even talking about?"
And somehow... we'd both understand perfectly.
The Lore™ begins here · External comprehension: 0% · Internal comprehension: total
Chapter VIII · The Calm Before the Boards
🍁 October – December 2024
Everyone around us was preparing for Class 12 Boards. Meanwhile... our chats somehow managed to include everything except studying. Talking itself had quietly become the reason we talked.
Studying discussed: none · Topics covered: everything else
Chapter IX · The Everyday Era
🚀 January 2025
Something changed. The conversations became longer. More frequent. You didn't need a reason anymore. A meme became a conversation. A random shower thought somehow became an hour-long discussion. One message could somehow lead to five different topics. Without realising it... chatting had quietly become part of my everyday life.
Conversations per day: increasing · Average length: unknowable · Reason required: none
Chapter X · The Great Blackout of 2025
🌑 February 2025
⚠ Incident on record · This chapter is part of The Lore™. Permanently.
Every friendship has that one chapter. This was ours. Up until 16 February 2025, everything was perfectly normal. Then... Nitya looked at both Instagram and WhatsApp... ...and apparently thought:
"Why block him on one platform when I can be efficient?"
So she blocked me. On both. Overachiever behaviour, honestly. One moment we were talking. The next moment... I had successfully lost access to 100% of the available communication channels. That's what I call commitment. For a while, the story just... stopped. Looking back now, I understand why it happened. Back then? I was far too busy wondering how I'd managed to get blocked on both platforms. I genuinely had no idea what had happened. So naturally... I contacted Aadya Jha. I contacted Paridhi. Both of them reassured me that you were okay... and both of them very strongly advised me not to go to your house. Because yes... my brilliantly thought-out backup plan at the time was:
"Fine. I'll just go to her house and ask what happened."
Thankfully... they talked some sense into me. About a month later, someone decided that maybe permanently exiling me from the internet was a little excessive. Communication slowly came back. The friendship resumed. And somehow... this whole incident went from being one of the most stressful chapters... to becoming one of the most unforgettable ones. Still... consider this your annual reminder that you blocked me on both platforms. I'm never letting you forget that.
Platforms blocked: 2 of 2 (100%) · Backup plan viability: 0% · This is officially The Lore™ now · File: permanently open
Chapter XI · Resume From Last Save
🌅 March 2025
Almost a month later... on 11 March 2025... we're finally back on WhatsApp!!!! No dramatic speech. No movie scene. Just... a message. Eventually, Instagram came back too. Surprisingly, it didn't take very long for things to feel normal again. The conversations slowly slipped back into their usual rhythm — almost as if the story had been paused rather than ended.
Save file: successfully loaded · All channels: restored · Time to resume normal: surprisingly short
Chapter XII · Like Nothing Happened
🌸 April 2025
This month felt almost surreal. The block became history. The conversations didn't carry resentment. Instead... they somehow became even longer than before. This was where texting started feeling less like messaging... and more like hanging out digitally. One topic became five. One joke created another. Random conversations became the best conversations.
Resentment carried forward: none · Conversation length: record highs
Chapter XIII · Building The Lore™
☀️ May 2025
Every friendship reaches a point where others stop understanding it. This was ours. Inside jokes multiplied. References started appearing. Entire conversations could revolve around something that happened weeks earlier. Someone reading our chats for the first time would've had absolutely no idea what was happening. We did. Congratulations. The Lore™ had officially begun.
External comprehension: 0% · Internal comprehension: total · The Lore™: officially operational
Chapter XIV · The Rabbit Holes
🌌 June 2025
This was where conversations stopped following rules. One minute... history. Five minutes later... business ideas. Then Assassin's Creed. Then politics. Then AI. Then some completely unnecessary invention that somehow sounded brilliant at 2 AM. Neither of us stayed on topic. And somehow... that became the topic.
Topics covered per conversation: uncountable · Average tangent depth: uncharted
Chapter XV · Welcome to Chaos
🎭 July 2025
By now... the friendship wasn't just between the two of us anymore. It spilled into group chats. People naturally involved both of us. Friends assumed we'd know what the other was doing. Voice notes became common. Stories became longer. Business ideas became increasingly ridiculous (from other's POV — I'm still in). The conversations evolved from texting... to full-blown episodes of TMKOC.
Chaos level: maximum · Business ideas: ongoing · Third-party comprehension: nonexistent
Chapter XVI · The Golden Age
🎤 August – December 2025
If someone wanted to understand our friendship... this is probably where I'd point them. This period had everything:
  • Ridiculous startup ideas
  • Fake businesses
  • Random midnight yapping
  • Voice notes
  • Callbacks to months-old jokes
  • "Tea"
  • Memes
  • Unnecessarily detailed stories
  • Absolute chaos
By now... we didn't really have conversations anymore. We had episodes.
Conversations: discontinued · Episodes: ongoing · Golden Age status: confirmed
Chapter XVII · A Shared Universe
🌠 2026 — ongoing
Archive status: open · Still being written
Years after that first
"Lab file bhej de."
the friendship had built its own universe. A single emoji could reference something from months ago. A random phrase could unlock an entire conversation. Someone reading the chats would think half the context was missing. It wasn't. The context wasn't in the chats anymore. It had become part of the friendship itself. And somehow... the story is still being written.
The Lore™: living document · Last updated: whenever you last talked · End date: none projected

Chapter VIII · The Letter

Letter

no tape. no doodles. just words.

Written sometime late. You know what time.

I've been trying to write this for a while.

I kept starting and deleting and starting again because nothing felt like enough. And then I realized that's sort of the point — that some things are bigger than the words you have for them, and you just have to write them anyway.

You became one of my favorite people without either of us noticing. One day it was small talk. Then it was 3 AM calls. Then it was the first person I think of when something is funny, or bad, or strange, or just worth saying out loud.

I'm glad for the unfinished plans and the debates that went nowhere and the inside jokes that require twelve sentences of context to explain to anyone else. I'm glad for the calls where nothing important was said, and also for the ones where everything important was said and we both pretended not to notice.

I don't have a neat ending for this. Which feels right, honestly.

You know the rest. You were there for all of it.
